# SES

## Acronym
* SES - Simple Email Service
* SNS - Simple Notification Service
* IAM - Identity & Access Management

## Doc

## Intro
* Send emails to people using:
    * SMTP interface
    * Or AWS SDK
* Ability to receive email. Integrates with:
    * S3
    * SNS
    * Lambda
* Integrated with IAM for allowing to send emails
